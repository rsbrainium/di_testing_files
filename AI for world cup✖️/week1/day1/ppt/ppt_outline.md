## Slide 9 — Key Takeaways

**Layout**: Title + numbered list

**Title**: Key Takeaways

1. AI drafts and surfaces — humans check for bias and decide.
2. Ground AI in approved documents; protect PII under GDPR/CCPA.
3. Prompt for one-off work; agent for repeat, consistency-critical work.
4. Agents need maintenance — a monthly review catches drift.
