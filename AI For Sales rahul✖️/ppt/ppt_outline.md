## Slide 9 — Key Takeaways

**Layout**: Title + numbered list

**Title**: Key Takeaways

1. Reps lose ~70% of the week to non-selling work — AI wins it back.
2. Coach the AI: context + instructions + examples beats a vague ask.
3. Trigger events lift replies sharply; act fast.
4. Personalize per persona, and verify every fact before you send.
