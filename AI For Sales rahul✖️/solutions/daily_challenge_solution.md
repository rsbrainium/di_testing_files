# Solution — 🏆 Daily Challenge — Win the Lumio Retail Account

---

## Part A — Scaffold and research

**Example scaffolded prompt:**

> "You're helping me, a Northwind Logistics rep. We sell freight-management software that reduces shipping errors and gives mid-size retailers real-time delivery visibility. Target account: Lumio Retail, a mid-size home-goods retailer that opened a second warehouse last quarter, is hiring a Head of Supply Chain Operations, and reportedly had delivery delays last holiday season. Produce a one-page brief: company snapshot, top 3 signals (with sources), and 2 likely decision-makers by role. Do not invent facts; mark anything uncertain as unverified."

**Example one-page brief:**

| Section | Content |
|---|---|
| **Company snapshot** | Lumio Retail — mid-size home-goods retailer, scaling operations (second warehouse). *(verify size/region)* |
| **Signal 1** | Opened second distribution warehouse last quarter — growth + multi-site complexity |
| **Signal 2** | Hiring Head of Supply Chain Operations — investing in ops maturity |
| **Signal 3** | Reported delivery delays during last holiday peak — active pain |
| **Decision-maker 1** | VP of Operations — owns multi-site execution |
| **Decision-maker 2** | CFO — owns cost/risk of scaling |

---

## Part B — Verify

| Claim to verify | Source to check | What would make you drop it |
|---|---|---|
| Second warehouse opened last quarter | Company news/press page, LinkedIn | No press mention and no employee posts confirming it |
| Hiring Head of Supply Chain Ops | Company careers page, LinkedIn Jobs | No live posting found |
| Holiday delivery delays | Trade-press article, customer reviews | Only a single unsourced rumor, no corroboration |

**Why this matters:** Each of these could appear in the opener. An unverified claim in a first message ("I saw you opened a second warehouse") is embarrassing and trust-damaging if it's wrong.

---

## Part C — Personalize for two personas

**Strongest trigger chosen:** the second-warehouse opening (relevant + fresh).

**Opener — VP of Operations:**

> "Congrats on opening the second distribution center. Scaling to two sites usually means delivery visibility gets harder right when accuracy matters most — we give ops teams one real-time view across warehouses so delays get caught before customers do. Open to a quick look?"

**Opener — CFO:**

> "Saw Lumio is scaling with a second distribution site — exciting growth. As shipping volume splits across locations, error and chargeback costs often creep up quietly and eat into margin. We help retailers keep those costs flat through expansion — worth a short conversation on the numbers?"

**Why these are correct:** Same product, same trigger, two different value angles — operational control for the VP, cost and margin protection for the CFO. Both are short, specific, reference a verified signal, and close with a low-pressure ask, exactly as the lessons prescribed.
