## Scenario

*Scenario (fictional):* You're still the **Northwind Logistics** rep selling freight-management software to mid-size retailers. **Lumio Retail** is heating up. Over the past quarter you've gathered several signals:

- Lumio opened a second distribution warehouse.
- They posted a job for a "Head of Supply Chain Operations."
- A trade-press article mentioned recurring delivery delays during their last holiday season.
- Their CEO mentioned "investing in customer experience" in a recent interview.

You need to decide where to aim and how to frame your message for the people who'll hear it.

<br>

## Exercise 1 — Persona value map

**⏱️ Estimated time: 18 minutes**

### Context

The same product means different things to different buyers. A CFO hears "cost and risk"; a VP of Operations hears "fewer fire drills." Your job is to translate Northwind's value into each persona's language — the way the lessons described tailoring a wedge by role.

### What You Will Practice

- Reframing one capability into persona-specific value
- Writing tight, specific value statements

### Instructions

1. For each persona below, identify the single outcome they care about most given Lumio's situation.
2. Write a one-sentence value statement Northwind could lead with for that persona.
3. Note one proof point (metric, example, or reference) you'd want to back it up — mark it "to verify" since this is a scenario.

## Exercise 2 — Prioritize the trigger events

**⏱️ Estimated time: 17 minutes**

### Context

You have four signals but limited time. Not every trigger is equally strong, and timing matters — the value of a trigger fades the longer you wait. You'll rank them and justify your call.

### What You Will Practice

- Evaluating trigger strength and freshness
- Defending a prioritization decision

### Instructions

1. Score each of the four signals from the scenario on **relevance to Northwind's product** (1–5) and **freshness/urgency** (1–5).
2. Rank them 1–4 for which you'd act on first.
3. In two or three sentences, justify your top choice: why this trigger, why now, and which persona it points you to.

### Deliverable Format

| Signal | Relevance (1–5) | Freshness (1–5) | Rank (1–4) |
|---|---|---|---|
| Second warehouse opened | | | |
| Hiring Head of Supply Chain Ops | | | |
| Reported holiday delivery delays | | | |
| CEO "investing in customer experience" | | | |

**Justification for your #1 choice:** *(2–3 sentences)*
