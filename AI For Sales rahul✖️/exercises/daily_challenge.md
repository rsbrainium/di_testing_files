## Instructions

### Context

*Scenario (fictional):* You're the **Northwind Logistics** rep. After weeks of watching **Lumio Retail**, you have a green light to open the account. Known signals:

- Lumio opened a second distribution warehouse last quarter.
- They're hiring a Head of Supply Chain Operations.
- Trade press reported delivery delays during their last holiday peak.

This challenge pulls together everything from the day: you'll scaffold a prompt, build a brief, verify it, and write outreach for two different buyers — the **VP of Operations** and the **CFO**.

<br>

---

### Your Mission

Take Lumio Retail from "target account" to "two ready-to-send, personalized openers," using AI to do the heavy lifting and your own judgment to verify and personalize.

<br>

---

### Part A — Scaffold and research

1. Write a scaffolded research prompt (context + instructions + constraint against invented facts) to brief an AI on Lumio Retail.
2. Capture the AI's likely output as a one-page brief: company snapshot, top 3 signals, and 2 likely decision-makers.

<br>

---

### Part B — Verify

1. From your brief, list the three claims you would verify before using them in outreach.
2. For each, note the source you'd check and what would make you drop the claim.

<br>

---

### Part C — Personalize for two personas

1. Pick the strongest trigger event from your brief.
2. Write a cold-email opener (2–3 sentences) for the **VP of Operations**, framed around their priorities.
3. Write a second opener (2–3 sentences) for the **CFO**, same product, different value angle.

<br>

---

## Deliverable

A single document containing: your scaffolded prompt (Part A), a one-page account brief, a three-row verification table (Part B), and two persona-tailored openers (Part C).
